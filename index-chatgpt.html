Election 2026 Overview

The 2026 elections will feature a wide range of candidates, ballot measures, and state-level initiatives. Voter participation is expected to increase, with several states implementing new policies to expand access.

Key Dates

Primary elections will begin in March 2026, with the general election scheduled for November 3, 2026.

Major Parties

Both major parties have announced their preliminary candidate lists, with more to come during the primaries.
